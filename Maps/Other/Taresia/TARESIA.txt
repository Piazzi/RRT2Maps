TARESIA 1875 version 1:

Welcome to Taresia! The Goverment has invited your company to build and run a railway system over a 50 year period.
Geografy: Taresia is a small tropical island. The main buisiness is mainly agricultural  and crops are rice (grain), 
citrus and bananas (produce), sugar, rubber plantations and some cattle. In the nortern part of the island there are 
some mining and logging industry. In this scenario, a some events will have influence on the outcrop ("Record grain 
harvest", "Record fruit harvest", "Locusts destroyes 50% of this years harvest").

Note:
As an attempt to avoid congestion, the train turnaround time is improved by 50%, traction +40% and acceleration +10%. 
Average distance between stations are 5-10 km.
