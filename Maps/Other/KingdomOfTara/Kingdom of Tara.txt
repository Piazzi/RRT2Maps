Kingdom of Tara 1862 version 2:

Welcome to Arboria! The Goverment has signed a contract with your company to build and run a railway system over a 30 year 
period. If you fail, your company will recieve a fine of 2 mill. The main buisines is mining and logging industry.

Note:  Space is reserved for quadouble track and/or bypasses around most of the big population centres 
Average distance between stations are approximately 5-10 km, using a scale of 2 squares = 1 km.

Win conditions: BRONZE: 50 mill., SILVER: 75 mill., GOLD: 100 mill. of company book value within 30 years,
And Tabora (the capital) connected to Finatere, Sina and Elguin. NOTE: No diesels avaiable before 1954 and no electrics 
before 1973. Special features: Four places  on this map I have tried to simulate tunnels, (steep narrow and overgrown 
gorges). Status over CBV, PNW, lenght of track, (single or double), and company type load hauled.
