SYLVANIA 1869 version 1:

Welcome to Sylvania! The Goverment has invited your company to build and run a railway system over a 30 year period.
Geografy: The coasts are subtropical lowland, mainly agricultural with dense population and the crops are grain, citrus and cotton and with 
some milk and cattle. In the hihgland, only the valleys are populated. The main buisines there is grain, milk, cattle, sheep, logging and 
heavy industry. In this scenario, a lot of events will have influence on the outcrop.

Note:  Space is reserved for quadouble track and/or  bypasses around most of the big population centres in an attempt 
to avoid congestion. The train turnaround time is improved by 50%.  Average distance between stations are 5-10 km.
