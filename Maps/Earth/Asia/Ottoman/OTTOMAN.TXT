Ottoman Empire 1885-1915
Scenario for Railroad Tycoon II
Version 1.0 [2002]

Author: Joseph Abbate

The Ottoman Empire at the end of the 19th Century - Can you bring it into the
modern world?

Start Year: 1885


Multi-player: Highest Net Worth after 30 years

Single Player:

      Gold: Net Worth of 20,000,000
      Silver: Net Worth of 12,000,000
      Bronze: Net Worth of 8,000,000



Ottoman Empire is based on Kees van der Griendt's map Middle East with a large
number of changes, additions and deletions to fit the period and scenario.
