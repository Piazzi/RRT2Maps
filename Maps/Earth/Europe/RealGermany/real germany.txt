
Thank you for use this Railroad Tycoon II map.


Installation:

Unzip this zip-file in the dirctory MAPS in your Railroad Tycoon2 Directory 
( f.e. C:\railroad tycoon2\maps)
Now start RRT2 and load this scenario.

This map is a map about Germany. You can play this map with 5 Computer-Players.
You can lay tracks only in the terretories of germany. You can't build in Poland, Danmark and so on...
You have many cities and many industries. I hope you enjoy this map...
